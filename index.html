Driving the Hybrid Revolution: Which Sports Hybrids Actually Deliver?

Written by Tyler Premnauth

Forget everything you know about the Prius. These three sports cars are the future of hybrid vehicles. Even though you might be skeptical about buying a hybrid, Hybrid vehicles offer superior fuel efficiency (often over 50 MPG), significantly lower tailpipe emissions, and reduced maintenance costs due to less engine/brake wear.
